# Film Holder Parts List

This list contains the parts required for the 3D-printed film holders.

Quantities may depend on the number of film holders and supported formats.

## Main Components

| Part | Quantity | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| 3D-printed film holder parts | as needed | Lower base and upper clamping parts |
| 2 mm × 22 mm cylindrical pins | as needed | Used for alignment between holder parts |
| M4 heat-set inserts | as needed | I used CNC Kitchen threaded inserts |
| M4 screws | as needed | Used to clamp or assemble the holder |
| M4 × 8 mm wafer head screws | as needed | Low-profile option for the film holder |
| Filament | as needed | PETG, ABS, ASA or stiff carbon-filled filament |

## Recommended Material

For this build, **Extrudr GreenTEC Pro CF** was used.

A matte black, stiff material is recommended because it helps reduce reflections around the scanning window and keeps the holder dimensionally stable.

## Notes

The cylindrical pins are used for repeatable alignment of the upper and lower film holder parts.

Screw lengths may need to be adjusted depending on the printed part thickness and the depth of the heat-set inserts.

Avoid glossy materials near the film window, since they can cause reflections during scanning.
